INSTRUCTIONS TO ADD YOUR PHOTO:
================================

1. Save your photo in this project folder
2. Name it: profile.jpg
   (or any name you prefer like: bhagya.jpg, photo.jpg, etc.)

3. If you use a different name, update line 177 in index.html:
   Change: <img src="profile.jpg" ...>
   To: <img src="YOUR_PHOTO_NAME.jpg" ...>

4. Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp

5. Recommended photo specs:
   - Aspect ratio: 3:4 (portrait)
   - Resolution: At least 800x1000 pixels
   - File size: Under 2MB for fast loading

Your photo will appear in the About section with:
- Beautiful animated frame
- Hover effects
- Gradient decorations
- Professional styling
